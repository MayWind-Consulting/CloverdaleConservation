Membership and Rules

We love our members!

Want to become a member?

  • Each membership year is measured from January to December.
  • You must attend a business meeting for membership approval (The first Tuesday of the month, 7pm).
  • You must have a Membership Application filled out for the Business meeting. You can pick up an Application Card in the Clubhouse at club events or print and fill out this document and bring it with you. (Docs link)
  • First year dues required when joining; dues are $100 to join, cash or checks are made out to "CCC".

Renewing your membership?

  • Print and fill out the attached document and mail it to the listed PO Box. (Docs link)
  • Annual dues are $75 and renew every January. Cash or checks are made out to "CCC" and must be included with the aformentioned document.
  • All renewals have to be in by the January meeting, if you miss this date you will need to re-apply as a new member of the Cloverdale Conservation Club.
Member Rules

  • Membership covers you, your spouse, children (18 and under), and grandchildren (18 and under).
  • Membership holders are required to be with qualified guests when visiting the property.
  • Our club operates for the enjoyment, safety, and security of all members; it is everyone's responsibility to manage the care of it and report any dangerous activity.

Range Rules

  • One guest only per membership.
  • Always handle firearms as if they are loaded; keep the muzzle of all firearms down range.
  • Never handle loaded firearms behind the firing line; No shooting while someone is down range.
  • No shooting glass bottles, bricks, or trash. No shooting at top spinning metal targets. Bullets must be deflected downwards.
  • Targets must be placed close to the backstop, as for the bullet to strike the backstop and not the ground.
  • No fully automatic fire is allowed at the range.
  • Shooting hours are from 10 AM to sunset.
  • Alcoholic beverages are NOT allowed at the range.
  • When two or more shooters are present, they both must determine a common firing line.
  • Shooters using the range shall raise the red "range hot" flag from the range house.
  • Club sponsored activities have first rights, and the ranges will be closed an hour before until an hour after club activities.
  • Under no circumstancess shall Tannerite or similar explosive products be used on the property/range.
  • Targets and trash, including spent cartridges, shall be removed from the range after shooting.
  • No vehicles are allowed on the shooting range; except as required by Club sponsored events.
